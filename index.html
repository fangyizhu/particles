Particle System by Fangyi Zhu

Cross platform runnable application is in git repository. Download and play with it!

Features Implemented

(15) Water Fountain

(15) Fire Simulation

(15) Magic Spell

(10) Gravity

(5) Bouncing on Floor

(5) Snow

(5) Art Contest Submission

(5) Realtime rendering

60 frames per Second.

frameRate(60);

(5) Video documenting your system and highlights features

(5) Interaction with obstacles

A blue box to play with, use up, down, left, right keys to move it around. Particles hit on it will bounce.

(5) Tails on particles

Particles in all modes have tails, long or short.

(5) Translucent particles

Fire, magic, snow mode have translucent particles, fire and magic's opacity change with lifetime.

(10) Real-time user interaction with system

(10) Multiple interacting particle systems

By clicking the last dot on up-left, turn on mouse mode. Particles will fly away from the cursor. Can be played together with the blue box. When moving fast, some particles can be trapped, it's fun. Showed in video.
